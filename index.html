About

A different kind of hackathon.

Get ready for a weekend full of learning new technologies, making great friends, and pushing your limits. We’ll provide plenty of healthy food, awesome swag, and everything you need to carry out your craziest ideas.

Any student can attend!

Whether you’re making your first website, hardware hacking with an Arduino, or starting with design, HackMTY is for you. We’ll have great workshops, mentors from different companies, and most importantly, the perfect atmosphere for learning something new.

Fee

Did we forget to mention it's FREE!

Yep there is no entry fee to participate.
